## Question 1

**Question:**  
TikTok's **new-user D7 retention** (the % of newly signed-up accounts that return at least once between days 1-7 after sign-up) dropped **12%** over the past 3 weeks in the United States. Diagnose the issue and recommend action.

**Metric Definition:**
- **Metric:** New-user D7 retention — % of US accounts that signed up on Day 0 and had at least one logged-in app open during Day 1 through Day 7
- **Measurement:** Computed daily on a cohort basis from `account_created_at` timestamp + `app_session_start` events; deduplicated by `device_id` to filter ghost accounts
- **Baseline:** ~52-54% for US new users over the prior 12 months; weekly variance typically ±1.5%

**The Drop:**
- **Magnitude:** D7 retention fell from ~53% to ~47% (≈ 12% relative drop, 6 absolute percentage points)
- **Timeline:** Began about 3 weeks ago; appears stable at the new lower level (not still falling, not recovering)
- **Pattern:** Roughly step-function over a 4-5 day window, then plateau — not gradual

**Product Context:**
TikTok is the dominant short-form video app in the US with ~150M monthly users. New user activation is anchored by the For You Page (FYP) — a personalized recommendation feed that's the primary onboarding surface; new users typically see a curated "cold start" feed for the first 1-3 sessions before personalization kicks in. The growth team owns activation; the recommendations team owns the FYP algorithm.

**Recent Changes:**
- **3 weeks ago:** A new "interest picker" was rolled out 100% to US new users — a 3-screen onboarding flow asking new users to pick interests + age before showing the FYP. Designed to improve long-term personalization.
- **3 weeks ago (same week):** A new cold-start ranking model (v4) shipped to US new users — uses interest picker output + device priors instead of pure popularity
- **2.5 weeks ago:** TikTok Shop expanded "shoppable video" coverage in the US new-user FYP (~15% more shop-tagged videos shown in first 3 sessions)
- **External:** Instagram Reels ran a ~$50M Gen Z paid acquisition push in the US starting ~4 weeks ago; YouTube Shorts pushed a US-only payout boost ~3 weeks ago
- **Macro:** US college spring semester ended ~2 weeks ago (potential seasonality)
- **Logging:** Mobile SDK v9.1 rolled out 4 weeks ago — analytics team flagged a "minor instrumentation refresh" but said it shouldn't affect retention computation

**Initial Data Points:**
You have access to the following data:

1. **Segment breakdown by acquisition channel:**
   - Organic / word-of-mouth installs: D7 retention −14% (53% → 46%)
   - Paid app-install ads: D7 retention −9% (50% → 45%)
   - Influencer/referral installs: D7 retention −13%

2. **Segment breakdown by platform & device:**
   - iOS: D7 retention −13%
   - Android: D7 retention −11%
   - Low-end Android (RAM < 4GB): D7 retention −22% (much sharper drop)
   - High-end iPhone (15 Pro / 16): D7 retention −7% (milder drop)

3. **Segment breakdown by age:**
   - 13-17 (US teens): D7 retention −18% (biggest drop)
   - 18-24: D7 retention −12%
   - 25-34: D7 retention −8%
   - 35+: D7 retention −5%

4. **Related metrics:**
   - **Sign-up completion rate:** −9% (more new users dropping during onboarding itself — interest picker is the new step)
   - **Day 0 → Day 1 retention:** −16% (the biggest single-day cliff)
   - **Avg session length for new users who DO return:** roughly flat
   - **% of FYP first-session videos that are shoppable:** +14% (matches Shop rollout)
   - **Crash rate on low-end Android during onboarding:** +3.5x (was 0.4%, now ~1.4%)
   - **Cold-start CTR (v4 model):** roughly flat vs. v3
   - **App-store install volume in US:** roughly flat

5. **Temporal pattern:**
   - The step-function drop aligns most cleanly with the **interest picker + cold-start model v4** rollout, not with TikTok Shop expansion or external competitor moves
   - The size of the Day-0 → Day-1 cliff (−16%) is much larger than Day-3 → Day-7 (−4%) — suggesting the loss is at the *very top* of the activation funnel, not later in week 1

6. **Other context:**
   - Engineering reports an open ticket: interest picker has a known issue where on Android devices with <4GB RAM, the third onboarding screen sometimes hangs for 8-12 seconds before the FYP loads
   - Customer support tickets about "the app froze when I first opened it" up ~4x in the last 3 weeks, concentrated on low-end Android

**Your Task:**
Use the S.I.E.V.E framework to:
1. **S**cope the problem (is the metric real, what's "D7 retention" in this context, what's "normal"?)
