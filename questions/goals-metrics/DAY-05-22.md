## Question 1

**Question:**  
Define success metrics for WhatsApp Channels.

**Product Description:**
WhatsApp Channels is Meta's one-to-many broadcast feature inside WhatsApp — admins (creators, brands, sports teams, news outlets, celebrities, political figures, governments) broadcast text, photos, video, polls, and links to followers. Discovery happens via an in-app directory and search; followers see updates in a dedicated "Updates" tab separate from Chats. End-to-end encryption is *not* applied (unlike personal chats), enabling search and reporting.

**Current state:**
- Launched globally Sept 2023; ~1B users have engaged with at least one Channel
- ~200M+ "follows" of major Channels (Cristiano Ronaldo, BCCI, Modi, NBA, Real Madrid, etc.)
- Monetization not yet launched; no ads in Channels, but the strategic goal is to eventually run subscriptions + paid promotion
- Heavily skewed to mobile, very strong in India, Brazil, Indonesia, Mexico
- Sits inside a 2.9B-user WhatsApp base

**Company Context:**
- **Mission:** Connect everyone in the world and give people the power to build community
- **Strategic priority:** Turn WhatsApp into Meta's third major surface (after Feed and Reels) — drive engagement, broadcast volume, and lay rails for a Business + Creator monetization layer
- **Competitive landscape:** Telegram Channels (dominant in this format pre-WhatsApp, especially crypto/news/torrent communities), Instagram Broadcast Channels (Meta's own — risk of cannibalization), X Premium broadcasts, YouTube community posts, Substack Notes

**The Challenge:**
This is a brand-new surface inside a massive existing product. The team has to decide what "success" looks like *before* monetization — because optimizing for the wrong proxy now (e.g., raw follows) will distort the surface in ways that hurt the eventual ad/subscription business and could damage WhatsApp's core trust. Channels also raises platform-health risks (misinformation, political amplification, scam channels) that don't exist in personal messaging. Defining metrics here is high-stakes: you're calibrating an ecosystem that 2.9B people implicitly trust.

**Ecosystem:**
- **Supply:** Channel admins (creators, brands, sports orgs, news, governments) post updates
- **Demand:** Followers consume updates in the Updates tab; react with emojis; forward to chats
- **Platform:** WhatsApp/Meta — wants engagement, future ad/subscription revenue, AND ecosystem health (no misinfo / spam blowback)
- **Adjacent stakeholders:** WhatsApp Business API customers (potential paid broadcast buyers), regulators (India IT Rules, EU DSA), advertisers (future)
